Welcome to the Missouri Medicaid Verification Portal

This secure portal provides quick access for applicants, employers, and Medicaid employees to manage and verify Medicaid applications and eligibility information.

Applicant Portal

Apply for Missouri Medicaid benefits, check your application status, and update your information securely online.

Employer Portal

Employers can verify applicant employment details and submit verification information to assist with Medicaid eligibility determinations.

Medicaid Employee Portal

Authorized Medicaid staff can review, verify, and approve applicant information to ensure timely and accurate processing.
