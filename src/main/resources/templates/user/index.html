Missouri Medicaid Applicant Portal

Welcome to the Missouri Medicaid Employment Verification Portal

Thank you for using the Missouri Medicaid Employment Verification Portal.

This portal is designed for individuals applying for Medicaid benefits to securely submit and track their employment information. By providing accurate details, you help ensure a smooth and timely eligibility determination process.

Through this portal, you can complete your application, update your employment information, and respond to verification requests from your employer or the Medicaid program.

Your participation helps ensure that Medicaid benefits are delivered efficiently to those who qualify, supporting access to essential healthcare services across Missouri.
